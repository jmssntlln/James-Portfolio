Biography

Who I Am

Growing up in Batangas, I developed a natural curiosity for how technology can make everyday tasks simpler and more efficient. I’ve always enjoyed figuring out how things work and finding small ways to improve the experiences people interact with. Over time, this interest led me to explore different areas of tech—from design and development to analytics and project work. Eventually, I found my strength in building clean, user-friendly digital products.

I’ve worked on projects like an AI-powered complaint and feedback system for DPWH Batangas IV, where I handled frontend development and UI/UX design for both web and mobile. I enjoy working with tools like React, React Native, Tailwind CSS, Firebase, and design platforms such as Figma and Canva. I value collaborating with teams, solving problems through thoughtful design, and creating solutions that are both functional and easy to use.

Along the way, I’ve earned recognition such as winning first place in the 2025 iPitch Competition and completing training in SmartBooks ERP and Power BI. At my core, I’m someone who values clarity, good design, and continuous learning—and I’m always excited to build, explore, and create things that help people in practical and meaningful ways.
