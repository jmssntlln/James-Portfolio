Portfolio Project

FITech: Barako Fitness App

Mobile experience for gym operations & engagement

Back to projects

Case Study

Transforming local gym management and member experience

Project Overview

  • Project Type UI Design · Mobile App Concept · Business Plan
  • Client/Partner Barako Fitness Gym, Lipa City
  • Project Year 2025 (Conceptual)

Overview

The Barako Fitness App (FITech) is a comprehensive mobile application designed to simplify gym membership management, class and event bookings, equipment rentals, and personalized workout planning. It serves as an all-in-one user-friendly platform, generating revenue through membership fees, bookings, and rentals, while significantly improving the gym's operational efficiency.

The Problem

Barako Fitness Gym faced declining memberships due to outdated, manual processes and poor member retention strategies:

  • Lack of Integration: Business processes, especially membership renewal payments, were done manually, creating inconvenience for members.
  • Poor Engagement: The gym lacked connection with members due to little to no loyalty benefits or structured promotions.
  • Unequal Promotion: Diverse fitness ventures (CrossFit, Zumba) received unequal attention, unintentionally prioritizing some over others.

The gym needed a technological leap to enhance convenience, boost customer loyalty, and ensure equal promotion of all its offerings.

Users

  • Young Adults (15-25): Need affordable options and in-app benefits to support inconsistent routines.
  • Working Professionals (26-35): Need flexible, advance workout scheduling and planning to fit busy lifestyles.
  • Middle-aged Adults (36-45): Need guidance and personalized advice (coach consultation) due to mobility concerns.
  • Gym Owners: Need a personalized dashboard to easily monitor sales, check important data, and promote all classes equally.

Design Methodology Highlights

Insights gathered from target customers (gym-goers) and stakeholders that guided the app's structure and feature set:

  • Inconvenient Payments: Members found the manual process for renewal payments "inconvenient and often overlooked," leading to missed dues.
  • Lack of Motivation: Customers reported "no incentive to stay committed" beyond the first month due to the absence of a loyalty program.
  • Scheduling Difficulty: Busy users needed a way to easily schedule and book classes in advance to prevent their lifestyle from hindering their workout routine.
  • Owner Monitoring: Stakeholders confirmed they lacked a centralized, data-driven system to monitor sales and ensure equitable promotion across all gym ventures.

Solution Summary

FITech delivers a seamless, technology-driven experience for the gym community. The app’s main features streamline gym management and enhance member motivation:

  • Personalized Dashboard: Central hub for viewing progress, scheduling, and sales data (for owners).
  • Online Payment System: Secure payment for memberships and renewals, with automated due date reminders.
  • Calendar and Events: Easy viewing and enrollment for all classes (CrossFit, Zumba, Pickleball), solving unequal promotion.
  • In-App Benefits: Consecutive Renewal Vouchers (e.g., free gear after 6 months) and a Digital Loyalty Card for walk-ins.
  • Personalized Workout Plan: Tools for members to create, track, and monitor their progress towards personal fitness goals (including BMI monitoring).

User Interface Design

Mobile App

Personalized Dashboard Members and Walk-Ins can create personalized workout plans, schedule activities ahead, and set personal goals using the dashboard that monitors progress.
Service Reservation Online membership and renewal payments are available so users can pay their dues easily. Owners have a dashboard to check sales and important data.
Activity Calendar A calendar of activities is present, notifying users of events for each fitness venture. Users can easily enroll themselves in scheduled classes and events.
Membership & Rewards The app offers in-app benefits like Vouchers, discounts, and freebies for every 3 months of consecutive renewal, plus a Digital Loyalty Card for walk-ins.

Technology Plan

The app is designed to be built using React Native. This choice ensures cross-platform compatibility (iOS and Android) from a single codebase, maximizing cost efficiency and enabling faster feature updates. Development would follow the Agile methodology to ensure flexibility, continuous user feedback integration, and the incremental release of features across various sprints.

This approach ensures the app is scalable, responsive, and aligns with the mission of enhancing accessibility and user engagement.

Project Learnings

Reflection & Design Outcomes

The main challenge in designing FITech was creating a cohesive system that effectively served two masters: member convenience and owner efficiency. The solution required careful information architecture planning to balance user-facing features (tracking, scheduling) with the business needs (payment, data reporting).

This project strengthened my understanding of product strategy—specifically, how UI/UX can directly translate into business metrics like member retention (via the rewards system) and operational cost reduction (via online payments). It highlighted the value of using technology to transform traditionally manual service industries.

Next Steps: Should this concept move forward, the next phase would involve creating a high-fidelity interactive prototype and conducting targeted usability testing on the scheduling and payment flows to validate the design before full development. Key performance indicators (KPIs) to track would be membership renewal rate and class enrollment rates.
